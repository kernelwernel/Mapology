**Mapology** is dedicated to producing accurate maps through Geographical Information Systems (GIS) software across a wide range of domains such as geopolitics, geology, various environments, and much more.

This is just a hobby project to stash all my maps. Some are for serious work while others are just for learning and tinkering with ArcGIS.
